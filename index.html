About

We fuse multiple senses and art forms into a single whole. Every piece of knowledge — from physics to biology, psychology to business — passes through the filter of this system. INNRBURIAL is a creative project spanning websites, photography, music, animations, and experiments that demand the fusion of art and technology.
It's emotional, raw, associative — built from streams of consciousness, images, and distortions. We believe most of life happens inside the mind, not outside it.
